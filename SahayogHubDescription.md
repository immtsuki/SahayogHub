\# 🤝 SahayogHub



\### \*One Click Solutions — Your Community, Unified.\*



> \*\*One click solutions. SahayogHub unifies scattered civic tools — Lost \& Found, Missing Persons, Emergency SOS, Fundraising \& Corruption Awareness — into a single transparent platform for communities..\*\*





</div>



\---



\## 🧩 The Problem



Right now, when something goes wrong — a dog goes missing, a road collapses, a child disappears, a community needs funds — citizens are forced to navigate a \*\*fragmented maze\*\*:



\- 📱 Posting on five different Facebook groups hoping someone sees it

\- 📝 Filing complaints at government offices with zero follow-up

\- 🔍 Visiting multiple platforms with no single source of truth

\- 📣 Raising awareness on WhatsApp/Viber with zero reach guarantee



\*\*The solutions exist — they are just scattered, unmanaged, and inaccessible.\*\*



ISahayogHub changes that.



\---



\## 💡 Our Solution



\*\*ISahayogHub\*\* is a unified civic platform that consolidates all community-critical services into a single, accessible application. Citizens can report, discover, contribute, and connect — all in one place — while maintaining \*\*transparency, accountability, and real-time reach\*\*.



\---



\## ⭐ Key Features 



\---



\#### 🔴 1. Missing Person Search



A searchable, uploadable database of missing persons. Citizens and law enforcement can \*\*submit and browse\*\* missing person reports with photos, last known location, and contact details. Fast, centralized, and life-saving.



\- Upload photos and key identification details

\- Filter by region, age, date reported

\- Share directly to social platforms from within the app



\---



\#### 🚨 2. Emergency Call / SOS Feature



One-tap \*\*emergency alerts\*\* to local authorities or pre-set contacts. Includes location sharing and incident type tagging.



\- Trigger SOS with geo-tagged location

\- Notify local police, fire, or medical services

\- Panic button for vulnerable individuals



\---



\#### 🐾 3. Lost \& Found



Citizens upload \*\*lost or found items\*\* — pets, belongings, documents — with photos and contact details. No more scrolling through ten Facebook pages.



\- Post lost/found with image upload

\- Tag by category: pets, electronics, documents, vehicles

\- Geolocation tagging and date filtering

\- Match notifications when a similar item is reported



\---



\#### 💰 4. Community Fundraiser



A transparent \*\*fundraising module\*\* for individuals in need — medical emergencies, disaster victims, education support. Every contribution is traceable.



\- Create and browse active fund campaigns

\- Show progress bars, donor counts, and target amounts

\- \*\*Transparency dashboard\*\* — all collections and disbursements visible to the public



\---



\#### 🏗️ 5. Road \& Public Hazard Awareness



Citizens report \*\*road damage, potholes, broken infrastructure\*\*, and public hazards in real time. Reports are geotagged and escalated to the relevant municipal authority.



\- Photo + location upload for any hazard

\- Status tracking: Reported → Acknowledged → Resolved

\- Public visibility to ensure accountability



\---



\#### 🔍 6. Corruption Awareness \& Reporting



A \*\*whistleblower-friendly reporting portal\*\* where citizens can flag corrupt practices, document incidents, and demand accountability from public officials — with optional anonymity.



\- Submit corruption reports with evidence uploads

\- Option for anonymous or identified reporting

\- Community upvote system for visibility

\- Forward to relevant oversight bodies
